FAQ

Common questions

What happens after I sign up?

We'll be in touch within 2 hours to confirm your plan, set up your platform account, and schedule your onboarding call. For creator shoot packages, we'll match you with a creator and confirm a date within 48 hours.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes — monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. You keep access until the end of your billing period. Creator shoot days are non-refundable once a creator has been booked, but we'll always try to reschedule.

Do I own the content that's created?

Absolutely. All content created for your business is 100% yours. You have full commercial rights to use, edit, and repurpose everything we produce — across any platform, forever.

What types of businesses do you work with?

All types — from sole traders and local shops to multi-location chains and growing brands. We've created content for hospitality, retail, fitness, professional services, property, and more. If your customers are on social media, we can help.

How do creator shoots work?

We match you with a creator from our vetted pool based on your niche and location. They arrive at your premises for a full shoot day, capturing video and photo content. Live GPS tracking is available so you know exactly when they arrive. All footage is then edited and delivered through the platform within 48 hours.
