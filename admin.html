How events work

After creating an event you can upload photos from the Upload Photos section.

Guests visit the event link, enter the code if required, and browse watermarked previews.

They can use face recognition to find their photos, then request a download.

You approve or reject requests from Download Requests.
30-day expiry
Events and all their photos are automatically deleted after the expiry date.
