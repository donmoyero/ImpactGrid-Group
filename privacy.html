Privacy Policy

How ImpactGrid Creator Intelligence collects, uses, and protects your data.

Contents
1. Overview 2. Information We Collect 3. How We Use It 4. Third-Party Services 5. Data Sharing 6. Security 7. Your Rights 8. Data Retention 9. Cookies 10. Children 11. Policy Changes 12. Contact
Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

ImpactGrid Creator Intelligence ("ImpactGrid", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by ImpactGrid Group Ltd, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it.

ImpactGrid Creator Intelligence is a web-based platform providing analytics, AI business insights, a professional network, and creator tools — including integrations with YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Name and email address provided at registration
  • Account and profile information including skills, location, and availability
  • Usage and activity data such as pages visited and features used
  • Business data you upload to the Analytics platform
  • TikTok account data — profile, videos, and engagement metrics (where authorised)
  • YouTube account data — channel stats and video performance (where authorised)
  • Instagram account data — profile and media data (where authorised)
  • Device and browser information for security and performance
  • Communication data from any support requests you send us

We only collect data necessary to provide the services you use. We do not collect data in the background without your knowledge.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our platform and services
  • Authenticate users and manage account access
  • Retrieve analytics data from connected social platforms
  • Enable content publishing via connected platform APIs
  • Generate AI-powered business and content insights
  • Match professionals with employers on the ImpactGrid Network
  • Send transactional communications such as account approvals
  • Ensure security, detect fraud, and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising profiling or sell it to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

ImpactGrid integrates with the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review:

  • TikTok — Authentication, analytics, and content publishing via TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API
  • YouTube — Channel analytics and video performance data via YouTube Data API v3
  • Instagram — Profile, media, and insights data via Instagram Basic Display API
  • Supabase — Secure database hosting and user authentication infrastructure
  • Cloudflare — Security, performance, and DDoS protection
  • Google Fonts — Typography delivery via Google's CDN

Where we access third-party platform data on your behalf, we only request the minimum permissions necessary and only retain that data for as long as required to provide the service.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the platform, under strict data processing agreements
  • With employers on the ImpactGrid Network when you apply for a role (limited to your profile information)
  • When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to you

Any third party who receives data is contractually required to protect it in accordance with applicable data protection law.

6. Security

We take the security of your data seriously and apply industry-standard measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure password hashing via Supabase Auth
  • Access controls limiting data to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of platform integrations

While we apply rigorous security practices, no system is completely impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at support@impactgridgroup.com.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law (including the UK GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@impactgridgroup.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the services you use, or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Third-party platform tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time from your dashboard
  • Business analytics data you upload is retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Application and job data on the Network is retained for up to 12 months after a role is closed

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support@impactgridgroup.com.

9. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on the platform. Specifically, we use:

  • Essential cookies — required for authentication and session management
  • Preference cookies — to remember settings such as your chosen theme (light or dark mode)
  • Analytics cookies — to understand how the platform is used and improve it

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the platform.

10. Children's Privacy

ImpactGrid is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify registered users by email.

Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
