Privacy Policy

How ImpactGrid collects, uses, and protects your personal data. Last updated: 2026.

On this page
Overview Information We Collect How We Use Information Third-Party Services Data Sharing Security Your Rights Cookies Contact Us

01 Overview

ImpactGrid Group Ltd ("ImpactGrid", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the ImpactGrid platform, including our analytics tools, creator studio, professional network, and Dijo AI assistant.

By accessing or using ImpactGrid, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

02 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information including your professional details, bio, and skills
  • Usage data such as pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Publicly available social media data when you connect third-party accounts
  • Communications you send through our platform or to our support team
  • Device and browser information for security and optimisation purposes

03 How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ImpactGrid platform and services
  • Deliver analytics insights, performance reports, and AI-generated recommendations
  • Generate content ideas, captions, hashtags, and strategic guidance via Dijo AI
  • Facilitate connections between professionals and employers on the ImpactGrid Network
  • Send platform updates, security notices, and relevant communications
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorised activity

04 Third-Party Services

ImpactGrid may integrate with third-party platforms and services to enhance functionality. These include:

  • TikTok — for content analytics and creator performance data
  • YouTube — for video performance metrics and audience insights
  • Other social media platforms for publicly available analytics data
  • Supabase — for secure database and authentication services
  • Cloudflare — for performance, security, and content delivery

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies independently.

05 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating the ImpactGrid platform
  • When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of ImpactGrid or its users
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets

06 Security

ImpactGrid implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and access controls. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

07 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Request portability of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

08 Cookies

ImpactGrid uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience, remember preferences, and analyse platform usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the platform.

09 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please get in touch.
