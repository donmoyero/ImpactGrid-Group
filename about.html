Background

How ImpactGrid was built

ImpactGrid started with a simple observation: small and medium businesses in Manchester were being left behind when it came to social media content. The tools available were either too expensive, too complicated, or required teams of people to run them.

We set out to build a platform that combined everything — a professional video editor, an AI that plans your content schedule, a calendar to track it all, and a marketplace to hire creators — into one seamless system.

Today, ImpactGrid serves creators, agencies, and businesses across Greater Manchester and beyond. Our platform handles the entire creator workflow: from signing up and getting admin-approved, to editing videos, scheduling posts, and getting hired through the jobs marketplace.

We're a lean, passionate team based in Manchester. Everything we build, we test ourselves first.
